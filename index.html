About Me

Who I am.

I was born in 1992 in Cascais, Portugal. From an early age I have started working on my grandfather’s workshop, where he passed me the passion for engineering and craftsmanship. Later on I would start studying Physics and Engineering in Lisbon (2010) and later go to Germany (2014) and the Netherlands (2016) to conclude my Masters in Physics.

It was on a later stage that I developed a passion for Art and the will to combine a good technical knowledge with craftsmanship and aesthetic ambitions. This came from various experiences I had in various Artistic environments – with my Pop band and all the design effort that went into it.
